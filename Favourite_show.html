This anime is about Yato,an unknown god and a girl Hiyori. Even though Yato is a god but no one ever need him so he been doing some volunteer work instead until he met a girl named Hiyori.
Daily Life of Highschool BoyDescription
This is a comedy anime about how highschool boy act in daily life. It focus on three bestfriend, Tadakuni,Yoshitake and Hidenori along with others.
